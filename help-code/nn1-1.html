  1. Reticular Theory

    Joseph von Gerlach proposed that the nervous system was a single continuous network rather than discrete cells.

  2. Golgi Staining

    Camillo Golgi developed a chemical staining technique that allowed neurons to be visualized in detail.

  3. Neuron Doctrine

    Santiago Ramón y Cajal used Golgi’s technique to show that neurons are discrete cells forming networks.

  4. The Term “Neuron”

    Heinrich Waldeyer-Hartz coined the term “neuron” and formalized the neuron doctrine.

  5. Nobel Prize

    Golgi and Cajal were jointly awarded the Nobel Prize, despite holding opposing views.

  6. Electron Microscopy

    Electron microscopy definitively confirmed that neurons are individual cells connected by synapses.
